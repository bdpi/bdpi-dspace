Privacy Policy

The users of Digital Library of Intellectual Production - BDPI can navigate the site without providing any personal information to third parties.

The Digital Library of Intellectual Production - BDPI has identifiable personal information only of teachers, students and public servants of the University of São Paulo ensuring the integrity and security of such data through appropriate technology.

The Digital Library of Intellectual Production - BDPI do not monitor or disclose information about user access, unless required to do so by court order.

The Digital Library of Intellectual Production –BDPI does not use cookies and therefore do not have access to information collected by USP sites or directed by links.
