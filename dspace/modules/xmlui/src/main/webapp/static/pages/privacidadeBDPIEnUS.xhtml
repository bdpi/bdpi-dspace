Privacy Policy


Visitors of the Intellectual Production Digital Library (hereinafter BDPI) can navigate around the services and products without providing any personal information to other parties.

The Intellectual Production Digital Library has personally identifiable information only for teachers, students and servants of the University of São Paulo ensuring the integrity and security of data through appropriate technology.

The Intellectual Production Digital Library (BDPI) undertakes not to monitor or disclose information about user access, unless required to do so by court rule.

The Intellectual Production Digital Library (BDPI) does not use cookies and therefore do not have access to information collected by USP websites or links.
